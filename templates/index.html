Welcome to the hangman game!!

You lose...he hangs!!

Here are the rules of the Hangman game:
  • One player thinks of a word or phrase, and the other player tries to guess it by suggesting letters.
  • The word or phrase is represented by a series of dashes, with each dash representing a letter.
  • If the guessing player suggests a letter that is in the word, the letter is revealed in its correct position(s).
  • The game continues until the guessing player either guesses the word/phrase correctly or the man hangs!
  • If the man hangs before the word/phrase is guessed, the guessing player loses.
  • If the word/phrase is guessed correctly then the man is saved.
